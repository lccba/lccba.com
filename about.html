About Us

So I heard that you want some chickens. Well, LCCBA is just the right hatchery for you! I know that we're not considered an actual "hatchery" because honestly, I don't have thousands of eggs and 20 XXL incubators in my chicken coop. So I'm keeping it simple. If you live in or around Peetz, CO (or select Sterling, CO locations) then you are eligible to buy and get some chicks or eggs delivered to your house!

What we do

LCCBA is a chicken business, willing to meet your poultry needs at a fair and inexpensive price. We also sell fertile eggs and normal eggs until your chicks decide to start laying their own!

Our Mission

Our mission statement: "LCCBA is willing to sell quality poultry, to local chicken lovers, at an inexpensive cost!"
Our Motto: "Quality Chickens, Local and Inexpensive"
Our Business Name: LCCBA stands for Lyle and the Coop Chicken Breeding Association
